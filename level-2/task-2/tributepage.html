Pawan Kalyan

Pawan Kalyan is an Indian film actor, director, producer, screenwriter, writer, and politician who predominantly works in Telugu cinema. He is known for his unique acting style and has acted in several blockbuster Telugu films, including "Kushi", "Gabbar Singh", "Attarintiki Daredi", and "Vakeel Saab", among others. Pawan Kalyan has also been involved in politics and formed his own political party named Jana Sena Party in 2008. He is considered a youth icon in Andhra Pradesh and has a huge fan following in Telugu-speaking states.
